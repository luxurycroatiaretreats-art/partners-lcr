Naša priča Our story

Nastalo iz frustracije.
Izgrađeno iz iskustva. Born from frustration.
Built from experience.

Nisam osnovala LCR kao poslovnu ideju. Osnovala sam ga kao odgovor na problem koji sam sama živjela — kao vlasnica vile koja je godinama plaćala velike provizije platformama dok je istovremeno gledala kako gosti žele direktan kontakt, osobni odnos i autentično iskustvo. I didn't found LCR as a business idea. I founded it as an answer to a problem I lived myself — as a villa owner who paid large commissions to platforms for years, while watching guests reach out for direct contact, personal relationships and authentic experiences.

Turizam mi je u krvi — tri generacije moje obitelji grade i žive ovaj posao. Znam što znači svaka rezervacija, svaki gost, svaki euro koji ostaje ili odlazi posredniku. I znam da model visokih provizija nije dugoročno rješenje ni za vlasnike ni za goste. Tourism runs in my blood — three generations of my family have built and lived this business. I know what every booking means, every guest, every euro that stays or goes to an intermediary. And I know that the high-commission model is not a long-term solution for owners or guests.

LCR je platforma kakvu sam sama željela imati. Direktna. Poštena. Bez posrednika. Izgrađena od nekoga tko razumije i stranu vlasnika i stranu gosta — jer sam bila oboje. LCR is the platform I wished I had. Direct. Honest. Without intermediaries. Built by someone who understands both the owner's side and the guest's side — because I've been both.
